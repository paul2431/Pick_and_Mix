Strengths

Weaknesses

Opportunities

Threat

Saturated market – more business like ours are emerging.

Place

The owners will use their house for the proposed location of the first Pic and Mix store where it will be located making it a home-based business. The owners will use a space in their house that has an ideal area for freezer space for on-site storage of frozen goods and an operating space that includes a counter and service area, burger grill, blender, and beverage display. This strategy was chosen in order to lessen the transportation fees and problems of goods and also to lessen the burden of the owners for the rental or leasing fee of the space that will be used for this business. Even though the location used is not the best choice for this business, the owners will use social media to advertise online and do online selling in order to catch or serve the target population or market of this business around Tarlac area to make profit.

Target Customers

Youth.

Kids and teenagers prefer burgers with different ingredients and also they like smoothies with unique and new flavors. What they like in our products is actually seasonal. Our products contain fruits which are actually good for them, especially the youth, they like fruit flavors more than veggies. Kids and teenagers will eventually enjoy our products, because our products are mainly made for them.

Adults.

Between 20-50 years of age, adults really want burgers, whenever they are hungry, they can eat burgers as their fundamental snacks. Also, our burgers contain new ingredients and flavors. Since then, in their times during the 19th century, burgers were already popular before, and it is affordable and in high demand. That’s why we made a new savored-burger so that adults will taste the new savory of the burgers that they prefer, like before. Smoothie lovers. Our products are destined to those people who love smoothies with a twisting burger. Since smoothie lovers prefer smoothie, our smoothie products are new and they can actually taste it.

Competitors.

Our business “Pick and Mix” has many competitors, especially nowadays that many businesses related to our products have been established. Some of our major competitors are Burger King, Angel’s Burger, Zark’s Burger, Cheesy Burger Factory Tarlac, Kerimoto, Gumalan Station, Juice shop, Jamba Juice, Lemon Chow, Johnn Lemon, Happy Life and many more. The products of other companies and sellers are quite similar to our products, “Smoothie and Burger ''. However, the difference between our products and the others’, is that our products are being picked and being mixed by the customer itself. Hence, in other businesses where customers choose their desired ingredients for their burger(which is already given in the customer’s menu), unlike in our products, they will be surprised on what taste awaits on their chosen ingredients, because they are the one who will pick and mix the ingredients depending on their desire. They will be curious about the taste of their chosen product. Which is very exciting and there’s a thrill in choosing the customer’s preference.

Barriers.

The products offered by Pick and Mix will be a barrier. This will serve as a challenge to the entrepreneurs because one of their products, smoothie, is inferior to the popularity of milk tea and milkshakes in their location. In addition there are many businesses who are offering burgers and smoothies thus gaining the loyalty of the customers. Lastly, due to the pandemic the price of the ingredients used for the products are inconsistent thus challenging the finance of the business.

Edge

The edge of Pick and Mix is it offers a wide variety of choices for its customers, compared to its competitors. Pick and Mix is a business which offers snacks and drinks relatively healthier compared to the competitors. Given the variety of choices a customer may choose from, the creativity of a customer is highlighted and this opens hundreds of possibilities for new products to form (since the products are customizable.) Customers will potentially buy from Pick and Mix because of the quality of the products, the ingredients, and the customer service. The employees of Pick and Mix are handpicked and trained to be able to face any challenges or obstacles that the business may encounter. The business aims to build a cleaner and greener environment, which is why eco-friendly packaging is utilized in its products; this means that when a customer purchases from Pick and Mix, they do not just buy for their satisfaction, they also help save the environment.
